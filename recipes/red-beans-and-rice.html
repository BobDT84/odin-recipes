Red Beans And Rice

Image provided by Ryan Kwok and sourced through unsplash.com

Cookware

  • Slow Cooker
  • Large Spoon
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Meidum Pot or a Rice cooker
  • (Optional) Pan

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of kidney beans
  • 1 smoked sausage
    • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 or 4 ribs of celery
  • Liquid shrimp & crab boil
  • Small bag of rice
  • (Optional) 3 bay leaves

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables
    1. (Optional)Sweat vegetables in pan
  2. Slice the sausage into discs
  3. Put beans and vegetables into the slow cooker
  4. Set slow cooker to high
  5. Measure out 1 or 1/2 cap full of liquid shrimp & crab boil, then add to slow cooker
    1. (Optional)Add 3 bay leaves
  6. Put the lid on the slow cooker, and cook on high for 4 to 6 hours. Stir every 2 hours
  7. 1 hour before the slow cooker is done, cook the rice
  8. Combine rice and beans on a plate and serve
